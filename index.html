Outdoor Learning Partnership 2025

Strategic Actions & Recommendations

Maryland Outdoor Learning Partnership • November 2025

Action 1 – Teacher Preparation & Environmental Literacy

Goal

  • Train all pre-service teachers to teach environmental & climate literacy across subjects.

Key Actions

  • Embed E-Lit in coursework.
  • Require 10 hrs outdoor teaching.
  • Launch Higher Ed E-Lit Report (2029).

Outcome

  • Graduates ready & confident to teach E-Lit statewide.
Environmental & Climate Literacy WG

Action 2 – Elevate Climate Literacy

Goal

  • Define the competencies of a “Climate-Ready Maryland Graduate.”

Key Actions

  • OLP Task Force drafts statewide guidelines (2026).
  • Map efforts & gaps; finalize framework Dec 2026.

Outcome

  • Climate literacy embedded across curricula & career pathways.
Environmental & Climate Literacy WG

Action 3 – LEA Facilities & Operations Gatherings

Goal

  • Quarterly gatherings for LEA staff to share decarbonization strategies.

Key Actions

  • 3 virtual + 1 in-person event at MAEOE Summit.
  • Co-host with MEA & MAEOE for content and outreach.

Outcome

  • 100 % LEA participation & progress toward Net Zero Energy goals.
School Sustainability WG

Action 4 – Align State Networks Supporting Environmental Literacy

Goal

  • Coordinate formal & nonformal education networks statewide.

Key Actions

  • 1-page summaries for MSDE, MELAN, regional hubs.
  • Formal communication structure linking networks.

Outcome

  • Unified statewide network strengthening K–12 E-Lit efforts.
Networks WG

Action 5 – Local AI Tool Initiative

Goal

  • Develop a local AI pilot to analyze environmental literacy data & career links.

Key Actions

  • Build pilot model + OLP resource database.
  • Show use cases & AI literacy training.
  • Find long-term host (agency or college).

Outcome

  • Secure state-managed AI system for Maryland-specific insights.
College & Green Careers WG

Action 6 – Revise the Maryland Conservation Careers Guide

Goal

  • Update 2020 Guide to add new industries & Youth Apprenticeships.

Key Actions

  • Include climate policy, renewable energy, carbon careers.
  • Create visual outreach materials for students & employers.

Outcome

  • Expanded career pathways and greater student awareness.
College & Green Careers WG

Action 7 – Outdoor Learning Data Initiative

Goal

  • Ensure equitable access to outdoor learning for all students via statewide data collection.

Key Actions

  • Form Ad Hoc Data Committee to identify efforts & gaps.
  • Compile provider/LEA data and publish report.

Outcome

  • Comprehensive picture of access & equity in outdoor learning.
Access to Nature WG

Recommendation 1 – Teacher Preparation Programs

Goal

  • Embed environmental & climate literacy in all teacher preparation programs.

Key Steps

  • Revise policy to integrate E-Lit & outdoor practice.
  • Annual Higher Ed Reports starting 2029.

Outcome

  • Every teacher prep program includes E-Lit by 2029.
Environmental & Climate Literacy WG

Recommendation 2 – Recognizing Sustainable Schools

Goal

  • Launch a Maryland Sustainable Schools Week to celebrate Green Schools.

Rationale

  • Advances Maryland Green Schools Act (2019) & Climate Solutions Now Act.
  • Reinforces student well-being and community engagement.

Outcome

  • Statewide recognition and growth in Green Schools participation.
Governor’s Office / School Sustainability WG

Recommendation 3 – Environmental Literacy Specialist at MSDE

Goal

  • Hire and maintain an E-Lit Specialist to coordinate statewide programs.

Alignment

  • Supports COMAR 13A.04.17.01 and EO 01.01.2024.15.
  • Reinforces Chesapeake Bay Program Outcomes.

Outcome

  • Position filled by 2026 — restored statewide capacity for E-Lit excellence.
Networks WG / MSDE

Recommendation 4 – CTE Pathway in Environmental Management & Technology

Goal

  • Create CTE pathway for careers in Green, Blue & Carbon Circular Economies.

Key Actions

  • Two-year pathway with industry partnerships and apprenticeships.
  • Align with Maryland workforce & climate goals.

Outcome

  • Expanded green career pipeline and growth in certifications.
College & Green Careers WG
